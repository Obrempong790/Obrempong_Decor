About Us

One of Ghana's most renowned and acclaimed interior design firms, Obrempong Design has a keen eye for the minor details and a desire for producing beautiful results.

With over 10 + years of experience, we have built trusting relationships with clients both domestically and abroad.

Obrempong Design is dedicated to delivering eye-catching designs that carefully examines purpose, pleasure, and beauty. It is also committed to style, inventiveness, and uncompromised service. We strive to create the ideal combination of modern chic and timeless finishes that represents all user personas and interests of individuals by taking a strong interest in emerging and current trends.
